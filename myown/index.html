ВАЗ 2101: Where everything begins.
Ya heard me right. Today, we will talk about VAZ 2101.
Basing off very famous Fiat 124, this car started sucessful series of VAZ (now known as Lada).
Car was a FR type (engine in front, working wheels at back), which became a sort of tradition in today's russian cars.
Original 2101's speed was 142 km/h, or 88,2 miles per hour for our american friends.
Started developing from good ol' 1970, its life ended in 1988 when its development stopped due to its outdation and in favor of much newer counterpart VAZ 2107.
It's mass was just 946 kg, or 2085 pounds.
Its fuel tank could lift 39 liters, or 10,3 american gallons/8,5 imperial gallons.
It also has a folk name "Kopeyka", or "Penny" if interpritated into english.
